## About

I studied Business Administration & Data Analytics at the University of Navarra, with time at the University of Edinburgh and IESE Business School along the way. Since then I've moved between energy analytics, geospatial ML, and marketing measurement — the common thread is turning messy data into something a business can actually act on.

I'll start the MSc in Data Science Technologies at the University of Edinburgh in September 2026. Outside of work: I'm usually looking for a football pitch, learning carpentry, or slowly getting better at the electric guitar.
